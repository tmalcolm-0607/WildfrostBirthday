Frost Knight Sprite Requirements

Required files:
1. phase1.png - The Frost Knight's first form
- Large armored warrior
- Glacial/ice-themed armor
- Imposing stance
- Color scheme: Cool blues and whites
- Size: 256x256px recommended
- Style: Match Wildfrost's art style

2. phase2.png - The Frost Knight's second form
- More agile/dynamic pose
- Cracked/transforming ice armor
- Shows signs of power unleashed
- Same color scheme but more intense
- Size: 256x256px recommended
- Style: Match Wildfrost's art style

3. bg.png - Background for both forms
- Icy throne room or frozen battlefield
- Subtle details to not overpower the character
- Cool, misty atmosphere
- Size: Match card background dimensions
- Style: Match Wildfrost's background art style

Note: All sprites should be transparent PNGs and follow Wildfrost's signature art style.
